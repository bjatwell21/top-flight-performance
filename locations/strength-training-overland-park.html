Serving Overland Park

Strength Training Built
for Baseball Athletes

Overland Park athletes competing at Blue Valley, BV North, SM East, SM South, and throughout Johnson County need more than generic gym programming. They need strength work that's specifically built for the demands of baseball — developing explosive power, rotational strength, and the physical durability to perform at a high level deep into a season.

Top Flight Performance's strength training program is backed by a Master's Degree in Exercise Science and CSCS certification. Every program is delivered through the TrainHeroic app, so Overland Park athletes have a structured, accountable plan that goes with them every single day — not just when they're at the facility.

And because TFP combines strength training with hitting instruction and speed development, everything works together. The strength work is designed around what we're building in the cage. The result is an athlete who's physically superior and technically developed — and that's a combination that wins.
