The TFP Difference

We Don't Just Train The Swing

Most hitting facilities will sell you a lesson pack, put your athlete in a cage, and send them home. At Top Flight Performance, we do things differently.

Our model is built around one simple belief — random lessons don't create elite athletes. Structured, intentional development does. We develop the complete athlete, not just the swing.

Meet The Coaches
01

2-on-1 Coaching Format

Every athlete gets real, individualized attention every single session — not lost in a group waiting for their turn. Two athletes, one coach, maximum development.

02

TrainHeroic App Integration

Every TFP athlete receives a complete training program delivered through the TrainHeroic app — structured workouts, strength programming, and skill development designed around what we're working on together.

03

Complete Athlete Development

We combine hitting development, strength training, and speed and power work because we understand something most facilities don't — you can't separate skill from athleticism. The strongest, most explosive athletes hit the ball harder and last longer.
