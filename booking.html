Book Your Evaluation

The Hitting Evaluation is the first step in building a long-term development plan for every athlete. This isn't a one-time lesson — it's a comprehensive breakdown of how you move, swing, and produce results at the plate.

What We Assess

During the evaluation, we take athletes through a series of drills and live swings to assess key areas of their game. We also evaluate how the body works within the swing, identifying any limitations in mobility, strength, or sequencing that may be holding the athlete back.

Using video analysis and real-time coaching, we provide immediate feedback so athletes and parents can clearly understand what's happening and why.

  • Swing mechanics
  • Bat speed and power output
  • Timing and pitch approach
  • Overall movement efficiency
  • Mobility, strength, and sequencing

After the Session

After the evaluation, we sit down with the athlete and parents to discuss results and lay out a clear path forward.

  • Strengths and current skill level
  • Key opportunities for improvement
  • Individual goals and expectations
  • Recommended training plan — hitting, strength, or a combination

Our goal is to give every athlete a clear roadmap for development, whether they're just getting started or looking to compete at a high level.
