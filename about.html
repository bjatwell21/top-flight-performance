The Coaches

Meet The Staff

Head Coach & Founder

Trevor Jones

Founder / Head of Performance

A four-year college baseball player at the University of Central Missouri (2011–2015), Trevor went on to have a brief professional stint with the Wichita Wingnuts before transitioning into coaching — bringing real, lived experience at the highest amateur and professional levels.

With over 7 years of coaching experience and a Master's Degree in Exercise Science, the approach at Top Flight is grounded in both science and real-world performance. Every program is built with intention — because what you do between sessions matters just as much as what you do in the cage.

Every TFP athlete trains in a 2-on-1 format and receives a complete training program through the TrainHeroic app, ensuring structured development continues long after the lesson ends.

Credentials & Background

  • Master's Degree in Exercise Science
  • Certified Strength & Conditioning Specialist (CSCS)
  • Champion Performance Specialist (C-PS)
  • 4-Year College Baseball — Univ. of Central Missouri (2011–2015)
  • Professional Baseball — Wichita Wingnuts
  • 7+ Years Coaching Experience

Hitting Coordinator

Zach McCloud

TFP Hitting Coordinator

Zach brings an elite playing background and a deep understanding of what it takes to develop as a hitter at every level. Having played college baseball at Mid-America Nazarene (2011–2014), he understands first-hand the work and discipline it takes to compete at the collegiate level.

Before joining TFP, Zach served as Hitting Coach at Hesston College for two seasons (2021–2022), where the offense broke numerous single-season records. He then moved on to MidAmerica Nazarene in 2023, where the team reached the World Series for only the second time in program history — and won their first-ever game at the World Series.

Since coaching at the college level, Zach has had the opportunity to work with hitters at every level — youth, high school, college, independent league, Minor League, and MLB-experienced hitters. As TFP's Hitting Coordinator, he brings that depth of experience into the cage every single session.

Credentials & Background

  • TFP Hitting Coordinator
  • Hitting Coach — MidAmerica Nazarene (2023, World Series)
  • Hitting Coach — Hesston College (2021–2022)
  • Certified Personal Trainer — NASM
  • College Baseball — Mid-America Nazarene (2011–2014)
  • Experience with Youth through MLB-Level Hitters
