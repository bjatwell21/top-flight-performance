Baseball Development Program

Kansas City has no shortage of hitting facilities and training options. What it lacks is a truly complete, integrated baseball development system — one where hitting instruction, strength programming, and speed development all work together around the same athlete. That's what Top Flight Performance offers, and it's just 25 minutes from Kansas City in Olathe.

KC metro athletes who are serious about taking their game to the next level — whether that's a high school roster spot, a college scholarship, or professional competition — train at TFP because the level of development here is genuinely different. The 2-on-1 coaching model, the TrainHeroic app programming, and the complete athlete philosophy add up to a system that works.

From middle school players building their foundation to college and professional athletes sharpening their skills, Top Flight Performance serves every level of the game across the Kansas City metro.
