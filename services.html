Strength Training

Skill and athleticism are not separate — and at TFP, we don't treat them that way. Our strength programming is specifically designed for baseball athletes, focused on building the power, durability, and explosiveness that translates directly to performance on the field.

Athletic Development

Speed Training

Speed is a skill — and like any skill, it can be trained. Our speed development program targets acceleration, first-step quickness, and overall athleticism to help athletes move faster and more efficiently in every direction.

We understand that the strongest, most explosive athletes are the ones who last longer and perform at a higher level. Speed work is a core part of developing the complete athlete.
