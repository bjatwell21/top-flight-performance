Gardner-Edgerton High School athletes and players throughout south Johnson County are 15 minutes from one of the KC metro's premier complete baseball development programs. The short drive to Olathe puts Gardner athletes in an environment where hitting instruction, strength training, and speed development all work together — not as separate programs, but as one integrated system.

If you're a Gardner athlete who's serious about the next level, the path starts with development that matches your ambition. TFP is 15 minutes away. The question is what you do with it.
