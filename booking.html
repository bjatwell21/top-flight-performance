Before You Book

What to Expect at Your Evaluation

Two evaluation types depending on your focus — tap a tab to see what each one includes.

Our hitting evaluation is designed to give athletes a clear, data-driven understanding of their swing and how to improve it.

1

Swing Analysis

We break down the athlete's swing to identify strengths, inefficiencies, and key areas for development.

2

Video Breakdown

Using detailed video analysis, we provide visual feedback so athletes can see exactly what's happening in their swing and better understand the adjustments needed.

3

Metrics

We track important performance data such as exit velocity, launch angle, and ball flight to give objective insight into performance and progress.

After the evaluation, we walk through everything step-by-step and build a plan to help the athlete develop a more efficient, powerful, and consistent swing.

Our comprehensive assessment is designed to identify exactly where an athlete stands and how we can help them improve.

1

Movement Assessment

We evaluate overall movement quality to identify limitations, imbalances, and areas for improvement. This helps us build a safe and effective training plan tailored to the athlete.

2

10-Yard Sprint

This test measures acceleration and explosive power — key components for performance on the field.

3

Force Plate Assessment

Using advanced technology, we analyze power output, vertical jump height, and Reactive Strength Index (RSI) to get a detailed look at an athlete's explosiveness and efficiency.

From there, we break everything down and create a personalized plan to help each athlete move better, get faster, and become more powerful.
