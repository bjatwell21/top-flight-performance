Serving the KC Metro

Strength Training Built
Specifically for Baseball

Generic gym programming doesn't develop elite baseball athletes. The demands of the game are specific — rotational power, hip-to-shoulder separation, explosive first steps, and the physical durability to perform at a high level across a full season. The training has to match those demands.

At Top Flight Performance, Kansas City area baseball athletes get strength and conditioning programming that's built by a staff with a Master's Degree in Exercise Science and CSCS certification — and delivered through the TrainHeroic app so athletes have a structured plan every single day, not just during in-person sessions.

The 25-minute drive from Kansas City to TFP in Olathe puts KC athletes into one of the most complete baseball development environments in the entire metro. Strength training doesn't exist in isolation here — it's integrated with hitting instruction and speed development to build athletes who are dominant at every level.
